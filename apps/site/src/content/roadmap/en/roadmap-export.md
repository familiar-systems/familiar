Quest 1: Session Zero (completed)

- Arbitrary things created by arbitrary templates referenced via arbitrary relationships form a usable, searchable graph-based wiki by both humans and LLMs.
- Initial audio extraction to wiki generation pipeline working.
- Initial LLM chat interface working.
- One GM per campaign/world.
- Campaign isolation: each campaign/world has its own workspace with its own database.
- Data for your campaign/world is exportable in a single click.
- Hardcoded system starter templates. Updatable via Pull Request on GitHub.
- Product quality evals in-place to track algorithm efficacy. We can figure out when we do a bad job and do a better one next time.
- Full GDPR compliance - no exported data, no cookies, nothing untoward.

Sidequest 1.1: Scry Company (completed)

- Public quality metric dashboards.
- Public financials.

Quest 2: Multiplayer (completed)

- Multiple GMs with multiple players.
- Invite players to view campaign content.
- Role-based access and viewing (GM vs. player).
- Watch your party edit the wiki together in real time.

Quest 3: Unseen Servant (completed)

- Have a Discord bot join your gameplay sessions on Discord. No upload ritual to remember.
- One person per microphone.

Sidequest 3.1: Per-Player Voice Embeddings (completed)

- Remember the vocal 'fingerprint' of each player in the campaign for higher quality audio transcriptions.

Quest 4: Move Basecamp (in-progress)

- Structured onboarding workflow for existing campaigns. Take your current, active campaign and bring it into Loreweaver.
- Support for single files: Markdown, OneNote, ODF, DOCX.
- Support for groups of files such as Obsidian or Notion exports.

Quest 4.1: Wiki (planned)

- Support for open websites not requiring a login such as a MediaWiki.

Quest 5: Comprehend Languages (planned)

- Pluggable localization framework.
- RTL language support.
- Support for English, Chinese, Hindi, Spanish, Arabic, French, Portuguese, Russian, German, Japanese, Korean, Italian, and Dutch.
- Additional languages are limited chiefly by text to speech, diarization, and LLM model support.

Quest 5.1: Interactive Maps (planned)

- Drop pins on uploaded maps that link directly to things in your campaign: dungeons, cities, planets, regions, whatever.

Quest 6: Community Center (planned)

- Community-made and maintained campaign starter packs for your favorite system. Note: if you want immediate support, our starter packs are hardcoded on GitHub. Submit a PR and it'll be deployed upon review.
- Community-made and maintained AI skills for your favorite system or workflow.

Quest 7: Mobile Apps (planned)

- Don't just upload a recording, use an actual app to record for you. Nothing to forget, guaranteed to work or the app yells at you.
- Optionally, have players also record. Every participating player improves the audio transcription quality.
- Cast Message. Private player to GM or GM to player communication in-app. Both chat and dice rolls.
