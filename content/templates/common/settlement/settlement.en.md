# Streets & Sights

The first impression. The districts and how they differ, the landmarks, the smell and noise of the place, the one detail that marks it as itself and not every other town.

# Who Holds Power

Who governs, who actually decides, and who they answer to. The guilds, temples, gangs, or houses that matter, the tension between them, and how coin and favors move.

# Worth a Visit

The handful of places that earn a scene: the inn, the market, the temple, the manor on the hill. What makes each one a place and not a backdrop.
</player_visible>
